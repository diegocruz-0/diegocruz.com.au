Identity Architecture

Designed and implemented B2B collaboration setups for organisations requiring controlled external access across Entra ID tenants — including cross-tenant RBAC, Conditional Access policy alignment, and guest lifecycle management. These migrations involve careful planning around mail flow, directory synchronisation, and Conditional Access policy rollout to avoid disruption to production users.
